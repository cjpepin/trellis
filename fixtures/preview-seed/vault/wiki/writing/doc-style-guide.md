Internal docs should read like a calm briefing rather than a brainstorm frozen in place. It keeps resurfacing alongside [[Warm Product Language]], [[Assistant Voice And Tone]], [[Citation Hygiene]].

## Working notes

The useful pattern here is to keep the note compact enough to revisit quickly, while still giving the next session enough structure to build on without restating the whole backstory.
