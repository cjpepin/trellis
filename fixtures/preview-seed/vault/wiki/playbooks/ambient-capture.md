Capture should feel quieter than opening a blank page but richer than dropping raw transcripts into a folder. It keeps resurfacing alongside [[Meeting Distillation]], [[Retention Loop]], [[Founder OS]].

## Working notes

The useful pattern here is to keep the note compact enough to revisit quickly, while still giving the next session enough structure to build on without restating the whole backstory.
