Conversation becomes useful when the residue is shorter than the meeting and easier to revisit than a recording. It keeps resurfacing alongside [[Ambient Capture]], [[Support Inbox Patterns]], [[Onboarding Friction]].

## Working notes

The useful pattern here is to keep the note compact enough to revisit quickly, while still giving the next session enough structure to build on without restating the whole backstory.
