A support inbox becomes strategic once similar requests are clustered into product signals instead of one-off answers. It keeps resurfacing alongside [[Office Hours Format]], [[Synthesis Support Signals]], [[Voice Of Customer April]].

## Working notes

The useful pattern here is to keep the note compact enough to revisit quickly, while still giving the next session enough structure to build on without restating the whole backstory.
