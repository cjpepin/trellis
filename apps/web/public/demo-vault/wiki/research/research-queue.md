A queue is useful only if it narrows into the next few questions the team actually needs answered. It keeps resurfacing alongside [[Reading List Systems]], [[Citation Hygiene]], [[Local First Principles]].

## Working notes

The useful pattern here is to keep the note compact enough to revisit quickly, while still giving the next session enough structure to build on without restating the whole backstory.
